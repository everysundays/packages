Our Vision and Missions

Sharing images with
imagination

Providing information infrastructure and sharing limited healthcare resources
to help each member of society live a healthy life.
Implementing high-quality and convenient medical information services addresses the
social issues related to healthcare and supports each person's life.
  • Establishing new regional communications for medical personnel
  • Resolving the information asymmetry in medical services
  • Building an information infrastructure that enables everyone to contribute to the healthcare
  • Providing opportunities for consumers to use health services wisely
  • Developing an information cycle whereby society supports medical research
