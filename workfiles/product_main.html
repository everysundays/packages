The CUBE

Compact appliance that is available on rental bases and enables medical institutions of any size to assemble the PACS of a desired scale.
SSD storage ensures the high level of fault-tolerance. The constant monitoring system helps to detect failures quickly.

Smart-retrieve

The function ensures the high speed of image access while minimizing the number of disks used at the hospital.
Based on the data from EHR and ordering system, it prefetches from data centers in advance the image data that is expected to be requested for local use.

Security, Safety, Compliance

Storing information of the consumers is compliant with the guidelines of the Ministry of Welfare.
The stored data is geographically distributed across multiple data centers equipped with redundancy mechanism, encrypted, and protected using secret-sharing technology.

Zero-investment

Using our rental CUBE units is cost-effective and accounting-friendly. No need to purchase / renew / replace costly servers.
Scalable monthly contract is easily adaptable to increasing storage needs.

Maintainability

The best experience for your users is achieved by centralized cloud-based approach to software upgrades, system configuration, and constant system monitoring. The failure detection ensures smooth and safe system operation.
